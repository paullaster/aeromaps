about us

Aeromaps Consultancy Ltd is a firm registered in Kenya to offer environmental, health and safety consultancy services. Our team comprises of experts in various disciplines including: engineers; environmental specialists; geographers and GIS experts; enforcement and regulatory specialists. Our commitment to our customers is to deliver value through technical excellence, professionalism, timely delivery, and trust. Our dedication to building mutual relationships through participatory problem identification and solution implementation defines us.
